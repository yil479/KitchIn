A story begins in the Kitchen

Search food court

We've got what you need!

People need to eat to survive, but our busy lives make this basic survival need almost last on the priority list. Many times restaurants are not appealing options for food because they are not as authentic, convenient, and cheap as a home cooked meal. On the other hand, people who cook often cook too much food for themselves and end up wasting food because it is difficult to find people to share their food with or to finish it themselves. KitchIn hopes to solve these needs and redefine the dining experience and build up a supportive food community.
